Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you share your AI link.

What exactly is an AI portfolio?
It’s a small site where people can ask questions about your experience, projects and skills. The AI is trained only on the content you provide.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. You just upload your existing material and we generate the rest. You can customise the look and copy later if you want.
Is there a free plan?
You can start with a free version to test the experience. Upgrade when you want your own domain, branding and higher limits.
How long does setup take?
Most people create a shareable link in under 5 minutes. The slowest part is usually finding your PDF resume.
